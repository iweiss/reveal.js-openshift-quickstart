Keywords

What to ask for?


  • Performance? Slow server?
    • Garbage Collection(GC) log.
  • OutOfMemoryError:
    • Out of heap? Heap dump
    • Out of PermGen? GC log
    • Native threads? Nothing Java specific
  • Lockup? High CPU? Slow startup/shutdown?
    • Thread dump
